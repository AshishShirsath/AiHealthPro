Diseases Our Web App Can Predict

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs either when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces.

Symptoms
  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Extreme hunger
  • Blurred vision
Heart Disease

Heart disease includes conditions affecting the heart, such as blood vessel disease and heart rhythm problems.

Symptoms
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in the neck, jaw, throat, or back
  • Coldness in your legs
Liver Disease

Liver disease symptoms often include swelling, pain, and yellowing of the skin and eyes. Hepatitis is the most common condition.

Symptoms
  • Yellow skin and eyes
  • Swollen ankles or legs
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
Chronic Kidney Disease

Chronic kidney disease involves the gradual loss of kidney function, leading to dangerous levels of fluid and wastes in the body.

Symptoms
  • Nausea
  • Loss of appetite
  • Fatigue
  • High blood pressure
Breast Cancer

Breast cancer develops in breast cells and can occur in both men and women, though it's more common in women.

Symptoms
  • A lump or mass in the breast
  • Changes in breast size or appearance
  • Breast pain
  • Skin dimpling
